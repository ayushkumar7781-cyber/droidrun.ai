DroidRun AI

Overview:
DroidRun AI is a simple AI-based automation concept that can run tasks based on user commands.

Problem:
Many users want automation but do not know coding.

Solution:
DroidRun AI accepts simple instructions and executes predefined actions.

Features:
- Task execution via commands
- Beginner-friendly concept
- Scalable for future AI integration

Future Scope:
- Voice commands
- Mobile integration
- Smarter AI decision making
